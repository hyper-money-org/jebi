# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want jebi to be a welcoming project for everyone. Whether you're filing a bug, submitting a PR, or joining a discussion, we ask that you treat everyone with respect.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be kind and constructive in feedback and discussions
- Welcome people of all experience levels
- Accept that others may have different approaches — disagree without being disagreeable
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissing or belittling contributions based on experience level
- Spam, trolling, or deliberately derailing discussions

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please open a private issue or email the maintainer directly. All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or ban participants who repeatedly violate this code of conduct.

---

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
